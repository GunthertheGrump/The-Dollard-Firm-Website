About The Dollard Firm

For more than 25 years, attorney Andrew J. Dollard has been committed to protecting the rights and freedoms of clients throughout Indiana. The Dollard Firm is built on the belief that every person deserves a strong defense and a fair fight in court.

Meet Andrew J. Dollard

Andrew J. Dollard is an experienced criminal defense attorney and the founding member of The Dollard Firm LLC, based in Noblesville, Indiana. Licensed to practice law in Indiana since 2010, he has built a reputation for aggressive advocacy in high-stakes criminal litigation, including drug offenses, DUI/OWI, and expungements.

Meet Nicole Krolewski

Nicole Krolewski is an Associate Attorney at The Dollard Firm in Noblesville, Indiana. A member of the firm’s trial team, her practice focuses on family law, civil litigation, and criminal defense. Since joining the firm in late 2025, she has worked closely with clients to provide dedicated advocacy and practical legal guidance across a wide range of legal matters.
