About The Dollard Firm

For more than 25 years, attorney Andrew J. Dollard has been committed to protecting the rights and freedoms of clients throughout Indiana. The Dollard Firm is built on the belief that every person deserves a strong defense and a fair fight in court.

Meet Andrew J. Dollard

Andrew J. Dollard is a seasoned Criminal Justice Trial Attorney with extensive courtroom experience dating back to 1997. With over 25 years dedicated to the criminal justice system, Mr. Dollard brings a unique perspective to his practice that combines law enforcement expertise with rigorous legal training. His practice areas include felony and misdemeanor defense, probation violations, community corrections violations, sentence modifications, licensing issues, and expungements.
