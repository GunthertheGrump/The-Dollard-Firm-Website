Personal Injury

Accidents can cause physical, emotional, and financial damage. The Dollard Firm represents clients in Indiana who have been injured due to the negligence of others, helping them recover compensation and rebuild their lives.

Types of Personal Injury Cases

Personal injury can result from various accidents or negligent acts. Our attorneys assess the circumstances and fight for maximum compensation.

  • Motor vehicle accidents (car, motorcycle, truck)
  • Slip and fall injuries
  • Workplace injuries and OSHA violations
  • Medical malpractice and professional negligence
  • Product liability and defective products

Legal Support & Compensation

Recovering from an injury can be overwhelming. We provide guidance and representation through negotiation, settlement, and, if necessary, litigation to secure your rightful compensation.

  • Investigation and evidence collection
  • Negotiation with insurance companies
  • Filing claims and court representation
  • Compensation for medical expenses, lost wages, and pain and suffering
  • Guidance through recovery and legal processes

Our priority is to help clients focus on healing while we handle the complexities of the legal process.

Frequently Asked Questions

What qualifies as a personal injury case?

A personal injury case arises when someone suffers harm due to the negligence, recklessness, or intentional actions of another party.

Do I need a lawyer for a personal injury claim?

Legal representation is highly recommended. An attorney ensures your rights are protected and increases your chances of obtaining fair compensation.

How long do personal injury cases take?

Case duration varies based on the complexity of the accident, negotiations, and court schedules. We strive to resolve claims efficiently while protecting your interests.

What damages can I recover?

Damages may include medical expenses, lost wages, property damage, pain and suffering, and other losses resulting from the accident or injury.
