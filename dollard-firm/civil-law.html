Frequently Asked Questions

What types of civil cases do you handle?

We handle contract disputes, property disagreements, personal injury claims, and other civil law matters that affect your rights and financial well-being.

How long does a civil case typically take?

The duration depends on the complexity of the case and whether disputes are resolved through negotiation, mediation, or court proceedings. We aim to resolve cases efficiently while protecting your interests.

Do I need a lawyer for a property dispute?

While minor disagreements can sometimes be resolved independently, having experienced legal counsel ensures your rights are protected and increases your chances of a favorable outcome.

What should I bring to my consultation?

Bring any relevant contracts, correspondence, insurance information, or documents related to your dispute. This allows us to provide a thorough assessment and strategy.
