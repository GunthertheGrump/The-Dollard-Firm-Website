Juvenile Defense

Protect your child’s future with experienced juvenile defense attorneys in Noblesville and across Indiana. The Dollard Firm handles delinquency cases, truancy, probation violations, and other legal matters affecting minors.

Delinquency & Truancy Cases

Juvenile delinquency cases can range from minor infractions to more serious offenses. Our attorneys advocate for minors charged with theft, assault, vandalism, or truancy, ensuring their rights are protected.

We focus on minimizing legal consequences while helping young clients understand responsibilities and navigate rehabilitative programs where appropriate.

Probation & Court Hearings

If your child is on probation, our attorneys work to prevent violations and provide guidance for court appearances. We help families understand court procedures and advocate for reduced or alternative sentencing options.

Our goal is to protect the minor’s record and promote rehabilitation over punishment whenever possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is considered a juvenile offense?

Juvenile offenses include acts committed by minors under 18, ranging from truancy to more serious crimes like theft or assault.

Can a juvenile have a criminal record?

Juvenile records may exist but are often sealed or expunged. We assist in protecting your child’s record and exploring expungement options.

How can I help my child avoid probation violations?

Compliance with court programs, regular attorney communication, and guidance on behavior are key. We provide strategies to prevent violations and protect your child’s future.

Are diversion programs available for juveniles?

Diversion and deferral programs may help eligible minors avoid formal convictions while completing rehabilitative requirements.
