Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a felony and a misdemeanor?

Felonies are more serious crimes that carry higher penalties, including long-term imprisonment, whereas misdemeanors typically involve shorter jail time and smaller fines. Our attorneys handle both types of charges.

Can a criminal record be expunged?

Certain criminal records may be eligible for expungement under Indiana law. We guide clients through the process to clear or seal their records when possible, improving employment and housing opportunities.

How do I choose the right criminal defense attorney?

Look for experience, courtroom presence, and a history of successful outcomes. The Dollard Firm has over 25 years of experience defending clients across Indiana, providing personalized legal strategies for each case.
