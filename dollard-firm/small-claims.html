Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum amount I can claim in Indiana small claims court?

In Indiana, the maximum amount for small claims court is typically $8,000. Our attorneys help ensure your claim is properly prepared to stay within court limits.

Do I need a lawyer for small claims?

While small claims court allows self-representation, having experienced counsel can improve your chances of success and ensure your case is presented effectively.

How long does a small claims case take?

Most cases are resolved within a few months, depending on court schedules and whether disputes are settled before the hearing. We work to expedite the process whenever possible.

Can I appeal a small claims decision?

Yes, Indiana law allows appeals under certain circumstances. Our attorneys can guide you through the appeal process if necessary.
