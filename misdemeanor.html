Misdemeanor Defense

Protect your rights and future with experienced misdemeanor defense attorneys in Noblesville and across Indiana. The Dollard Firm handles DUIs, theft, drug offenses, and other misdemeanor charges.

Common Misdemeanor Cases

  • Driving Under the Influence (DUI/OWI)
  • Drug Possession & Minor Offenses
  • Theft, Shoplifting, and Property Crimes
  • Assault & Battery
  • Disorderly Conduct & Public Offenses

Misdemeanor charges can still have long-lasting consequences on your employment, driving record, and personal life. We provide aggressive and strategic defense to minimize penalties and protect your future.

Our Approach

The Dollard Firm reviews every detail of your case, including evidence, police reports, and procedural matters. We negotiate with prosecutors, explore diversion programs, and prepare for trial if necessary to ensure the best outcome.

Our goal is to help clients avoid jail time, fines, or a permanent criminal record whenever possible.
