Malicious Prosecution

Facing legal action without cause can be damaging and stressful. The Dollard Firm represents clients in Indiana who have been subjected to malicious prosecution, defending their rights and seeking justice.

Understanding Malicious Prosecution

Malicious prosecution occurs when someone is subjected to a legal action without probable cause, with improper motives, and ultimately without success. Our attorneys analyze the details of your case to identify if your rights were violated.

  • Evaluation of legal proceedings and evidence
  • Investigation of improper motives or abuse of process
  • Protection of civil rights
  • Legal remedies for damages and harm
  • Consultation and strategic defense planning

Legal Remedies & Representation

Victims of malicious prosecution may pursue compensation or other legal remedies. The Dollard Firm represents clients throughout the entire process, seeking fair outcomes and accountability.

  • Filing civil claims for damages
  • Negotiation and settlement of malicious prosecution cases
  • Court representation and litigation support
  • Protection against retaliation or further legal harm
  • Clearing records or addressing wrongful legal actions

Our attorneys work to ensure clients are compensated fairly and that any unjust legal actions are properly challenged.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is malicious prosecution?

Malicious prosecution is a legal claim against someone who initiated a lawsuit or criminal case without proper cause and with improper motives, which ultimately ended in the defendant's favor.

Can I sue for damages?

Yes. Victims of malicious prosecution may recover damages for emotional distress, legal costs, lost income, and other harms caused by the wrongful legal action.

Do I need an attorney?

Yes. Malicious prosecution cases can be complex, and an experienced attorney can protect your rights and guide you through the legal process.

How long do these cases take?

Case timelines vary depending on complexity, evidence, and court schedules. Our firm works diligently to pursue justice efficiently.
