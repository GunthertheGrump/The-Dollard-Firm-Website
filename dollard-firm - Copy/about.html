About The Dollard Firm

For more than 25 years, attorney Andrew J. Dollard has been committed to protecting the rights and freedoms of clients throughout Indiana. The Dollard Firm is built on the belief that every person deserves a strong defense and a fair fight in court.

Meet Andrew J. Dollard

Andrew J. Dollard is a seasoned trial attorney with decades of experience defending clients in both misdemeanor and felony cases. His legal career has spanned hundreds of courtroom appearances, where he has built a reputation as a relentless advocate for his clients.

From DUI and drug charges to violent felony cases, Andrew understands the stakes for his clients and works tirelessly to secure the best possible outcomes. He is known for his preparation, his courtroom presence, and his ability to negotiate strategically when it benefits his clients most.

In addition to his legal expertise, Andrew is committed to treating each client with dignity, respect, and honesty. He believes in guiding clients through every step of the legal process, ensuring they are informed and empowered in their defense.
