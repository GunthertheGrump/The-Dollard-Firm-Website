Why Every Adult Needs a Will

A will is more than just a legal document — it’s a statement of care for your loved ones. Without one, your estate will be distributed according to state law, not your wishes. Our firm ensures your will is properly executed, valid, and aligned with your goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is having a will so important?

A will ensures your property and assets are distributed according to your wishes rather than state law. It also allows you to name guardians for minor children and specify personal preferences for your estate.

What happens if I die without a will in Indiana?

Without a will, the court applies Indiana’s intestacy laws, which may result in unintended heirs or family disputes. Having a valid will helps ensure your estate is handled according to your true intentions.

How often should I update my will?

It’s best to review your will every few years or after major life changes such as marriage, divorce, the birth of a child, or acquiring significant new assets.

Can I write my own will?

While handwritten or DIY wills are sometimes valid, they often lead to disputes or confusion. Working with an experienced attorney ensures your document meets all legal requirements and clearly expresses your wishes.
