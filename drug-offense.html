Drug Offenses Defense

The Dollard Firm provides aggressive legal defense for drug offenses, including possession, distribution, trafficking, and manufacturing charges throughout Noblesville and Indiana.

Types of Drug Charges We Defend

  • Possession of controlled substances
  • Drug distribution and trafficking
  • Manufacturing or cultivation of illegal drugs
  • Prescription drug violations
  • Paraphernalia charges

Our Approach

Our attorneys review evidence, challenge unlawful searches and seizures, and investigate every aspect of your case. We work to reduce or dismiss charges, protect your record, and safeguard your future.

We understand the serious consequences of drug convictions, including incarceration, fines, and long-term effects on employment and personal life. Our defense is tailored to each client’s unique situation.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is considered a drug offense?

Drug offenses include possession, distribution, trafficking, manufacturing, and possession of drug paraphernalia.

What are potential penalties?

Penalties vary based on the type and quantity of drugs, prior convictions, and case circumstances, and can include fines, probation, or jail time.

Can I avoid a conviction?

Experienced defense can sometimes reduce charges, negotiate plea agreements, or achieve alternative sentencing options to avoid a permanent conviction.
