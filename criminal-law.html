Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a felony and a misdemeanor?

Felonies are more serious crimes with higher penalties, including long-term imprisonment. Misdemeanors involve shorter jail time and smaller fines.

Can a criminal record be expunged?

Certain criminal records may be eligible for expungement under Indiana law. We guide clients through the process to clear or seal records when possible.

How do I choose the right criminal defense attorney?

Look for experience, courtroom presence, and a history of successful outcomes. The Dollard Firm provides personalized strategies for each case.
