Family Law

Family law matters can be complex and emotional. The Dollard Firm provides compassionate, strategic representation in divorce, custody, child support, guardianship, and related family law cases throughout Noblesville and Central Indiana.

Understanding Family Law

Family law encompasses legal issues that affect families and relationships. This includes divorce, custody and parenting time, child support, paternity, guardianship, adoption, domestic violence, and prenuptial or postnuptial agreements.

Our attorneys approach each case with care, ensuring your voice is heard and your rights are protected while helping you navigate emotionally challenging situations.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my family law consultation?

Bring relevant documents such as financial records, court orders, communication logs, and identification. This allows your attorney to assess your situation fully.

How long does a divorce take in Indiana?

Uncontested divorces can finalize in a few months, while contested divorces may take longer depending on court schedules and negotiations.

Can custody orders be modified?

Yes. Custody and parenting time orders can be modified if there has been a substantial change in circumstances affecting the child's best interests.

Do I need an attorney for child support?

An attorney ensures calculations are accurate, protects your rights, and ensures agreements are fair and enforceable.
